Awards & Top Games

Overwatch (2016)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Multiplayer, Best Game ESport, Best Ongoing, Best Game Direction

Overwatch was a 2016 team-based online multiplayer first-person shooter video game by Blizzard Entertainment

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (2017)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Game Direction, Best Action, Best Adventure, Most Anticipated Game

Set at the end of the Zelda timeline, the player controls an amnesiac Link as he sets out to save Princess Zelda and prevent Calamity Ganon from destroying the world.

God of War (2018)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Game Direction, Best Action/Adventure Game, Best Audio Design, Best Performance (Christopher Judge as Kratos), Best Narrative

God of War is known for its captivating storyline, exceptional combat mechanics, and top-notch performances.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice (2019)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Action/Adventure Game, Best Game Direction

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is praised for its challenging combat system and stunning visuals, making it one of the top games of 2019.

The Last of Us Part II (2020)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Game Direction, Best Narrative, Best Audio Design, Best Performance (Laura Bailey as Abby), Best Action/Adventure Game, Innovation in Accessibility

The Last of Us Part II is lauded for its emotional depth, stunning graphics, and exceptional character performances.

It Takes Two (2021)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Family Game, Best Multiplayer Game

It Takes Two is a cooperative action-adventure game that redefined the genre with its innovative game mechanics and emotional storytelling.

Elden Ring (2022)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Game Direction, Best Art Direction, Best Role-Playing Game

Elden Ring took the world by storm with its open-world design, intricate lore, and exceptional gameplay mechanics.

Baldur's Gate 3 (2023)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best RPG, Best Multiplayer, Best Community Support, Player's Voice, Best Performance (Neil Newbon as Astarion)

Baldur's Gate 3's' deep role-playing mechanics, engaging multiplayer, and strong community support solidified its position as a standout title in gaming.

Astro Bot (2023)

Awards: Game of the Year, Best Family Game, Best Game Direction, Best Action, Best Adventure

Astro Bot is a 2024 platform game developed by Team Asobi and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment for the PlayStation 5. It is a sequel to Astro's Playroom and the first game featuring the character to have been developed by Team Asobi since its separation from Japan Studio.
